Why Join Pineapples?

Pineapples is a place where members develop character and live by foundations of honor, integrity, and brotherhood. We have stand for excellence and set high expectations for our members. We push ourselves to strive for higher goals and to hold each other accountable to those goals. This brotherhood is an opportunity to find peers to challenge you to reach your fullest potential.

How to Join

Membership with Pineapples begins by attending our rush events. Existing members (actives) will then extend a bid to join. If the prospective member accepts the bid, he goes through a process that assimilates new members into the organization and prepares them to make contributions to the house and to advance the ideals of the organization. If you are interested in joining Pineapples, contact any of the brothers (you can find contact information at your school or on the chapter directory page at this website). Additionally, we will post rush information on our social media pages at the beginning of each academic term.
