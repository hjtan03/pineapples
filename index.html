Pineapples at University of South Carolina

About

Pineapples is a place where members develop character and live by foundations of honor, integrity, and brotherhood. We have stand for excellence and set high expectations for our members. We push ourselves to strive for higher goals and to hold each other accountable to those goals. This brotherhood is an opportunity to find peers to challenge you to reach your fullest potential.

The Pillars

  • Brotherhood
  • Academic Excellence
  • Philanthropy
  • Leadership
  • Asian-Awareness

Membership
